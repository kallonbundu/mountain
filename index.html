About, Us

Mountain View Villa is a luxurious retreat set in a picturesque location, offering guests a serene escape amidst stunning natural beauty. Designed for comfort and relaxation, the villa provides an ideal blend of modern amenities and nature's tranquility. Mountain View Villa is typically situated in a scenic area surrounded by breathtaking mountain landscapes, providing an ideal setting for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts. The villa's location offers easy access to hiking trails, nature walks, and other outdoor activities, while still being conveniently located near local attractions and amenities. .

Guests consistently praise Mountain View Villa for its stunning location, exceptional service, and luxurious accommodations. The villa is highly rated on various travel platforms, with many guests highlighting the scenic views, peaceful atmosphere, and high-quality amenities. Whether you’re looking for a romantic getaway, a family vacation, or an adventure-filled holiday, Mountain View Villa promises an unforgettable experience in the lap of nature. .

accommodations

Situated at 116 Tarawally Drive, Malama, Lumley, Freetown, Sierra Leone, West Africa

Mountain View Villa features a range of elegant and comfortable accommodations to suit various preferences: Deluxe Rooms: These well-appointed rooms come with modern amenities such as flat-screen TVs, free Wi-Fi, and comfortable bedding, ensuring a restful stay. Suites: Spacious suites offer additional living space, premium furnishings, and private balconies or terraces with stunning mountain views. Private Villas: Ideal for families or groups, the private villas offer multiple bedrooms, a fully equipped kitchen, a living area, and outdoor spaces, including private gardens and pools..
